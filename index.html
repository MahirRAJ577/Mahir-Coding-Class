CSO OLYMPIAD

Our Game

The basis of the game is a game that mixes fun and education where is starts you off with a menu screen with 3 options of natural disasters you would like to take on. The gameplay consists of 4 items you must collect to aid in your survival scattered across different rooms accompanied by a minigame afterwards to challenge you a bit and keep you engaged.

Instructions

Once you are on the main menu, choose the natural disaster that will be surviving. You can use the arrows on either side of the screen to move to different rooms. Items can be picked up by clicking them. Once, you have obtained all the items, you will begin one of the three (3) minigames depending on the natural disaster you chose.
